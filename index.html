Singing

[Event Code:E1]
* This contest is open to all
* You can add your singing videos in your post (youtube /facebook/ Instagram-igtv)
* Make sure you are using the following hashtags and tags on your post :@engineersconnect #engineersconnect #stackzeal #engineersfest20
* It should be public
* Participants are free to sing in any language and any genre.
* Maximum duration of the rendition should not exceed 2-3 minutes.
* Make sure that your voice should be audible and clear.
* It can be either a visual video or edit a video with a record of your singing along with images in foreground.
* When submitting the application, mention your full name along with the name of the song you sang.
* The participants are required to submit your post link in the g-form which we provide the whatsapp group, Therefore all participants are asked to join the whatsapp group preferred on this form.
* Selection : Based on Judgement and likes you received for your video.

Instrument Playing

[Event Code: E3]
* The competition is open to all
* All kinds of instruments are allowed- Wind,String, Percussion Electronics etc.
* You can add your videos in your post (youtube /facebook/ Instagram-igtv)
* A team can hold (solo/Couple/Group)
* Make sure you are using the following hashtags and tags on your post :@engineersconnect #engineersconnect #stackzeal #engineersfest20
* It should be public
* Video/Audio files must be performed by applicants and must not be edited in any way, nor should any effects be added.
* If audio alone is submitted, it should be of MP3 format. Participants are responsible for ensuring that video/audio is recorded with good quality(image and sound) and in peaceful surroundings.For video the instrument, face and hands should be early visible.
* Along with the audio, submit an image , like a movie image that matches with your work.
* The contestant will be judged solely on the basis of both the video/audio submitted and likes they got for the video.
* When submitting the application, mention your full name along with the name of the instrument you played.
* The participants are required to submit your post link in the g-form which we provide the whatsapp group, Therefore all participants are asked to join the whatsapp group preferred on this form.
Certificates will be provided to all participants.

  • Entries are accepted till 14-september-2020
  • Results will be announced on 15-september-2020

  • Submission Link
